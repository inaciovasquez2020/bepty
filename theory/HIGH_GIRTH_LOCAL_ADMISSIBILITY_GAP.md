# HIGH_GIRTH_LOCAL_ADMISSIBILITY_GAP

Status: OPEN

## Statement

Show that the bound-removal valuation scheme can be chosen inside the admissible BEpTy class.

Precisely, construct a canonical valuation V_r on rooted radius-r profile data such that

P_r(G) != P_r(H) => V_r(G) != V_r(H)

for all finite bounded-degree graphs G,H,

and V_r satisfies the BEpTy admissibility constraints.

## Role

This isolates the admissibility half of the remaining gap after
HIGH_GIRTH_LOCAL_BOUND_REMOVAL_GAP.
