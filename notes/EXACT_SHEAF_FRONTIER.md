# Exact Sheaf Frontier

## Open problem
Determine whether there exist connected simple graphs K,L and radius R such that
I_sheaf^exact(K,R)=I_sheaf^exact(L,R) but beta_prof,R(K) != beta_prof,R(L).

## Equivalent obstruction form
Determine whether kappa_R(K)=dim ker(pi_K) is determined by the exact sheaf package.
