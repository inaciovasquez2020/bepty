# Profiled Betti Separation

## Definition
\[
\sigma_R(G):=\Bigl\{\!\!\Bigl\{\ \min\{|C'|:\ C'\in [C]\subseteq Z_1(G),\ C'\neq 0\}\ :\ 0\neq [C]\in Z_1(G)/X_R(G)\ \Bigr\}\!\!\Bigr\}.
\]

\[
\beta^{\mathrm{prof}}_R(G):=\bigl(\dim Z_1(G)-\dim X_R(G),\ \sigma_R(G)\bigr).
\]

## Theorem
\[
\beta^{\mathrm{prof}}_R\text{ is strictly finer than the first Betti number.}
\]

## Witness
\[
G:=\Theta_{3,3,3},\qquad H:=D_{6,6},\qquad R:=1.
\]

\[
\dim H_1(G;\mathbb F_2)=\dim H_1(H;\mathbb F_2)=2.
\]

\[
\operatorname{girth}(G)=\operatorname{girth}(H)=6>2.
\]

\[
X_1(G)=X_1(H)=0.
\]

\[
\beta^{\mathrm{prof}}_1(G)=\bigl(2,\{\!\{6,6,6\}\!\}\bigr),
\qquad
\beta^{\mathrm{prof}}_1(H)=\bigl(2,\{\!\{6,6,12\}\!\}\bigr).
\]

\[
\beta^{\mathrm{prof}}_1(G)\neq \beta^{\mathrm{prof}}_1(H).
\]
